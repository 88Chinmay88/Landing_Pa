Pizza

A dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a topping of tomatoes and cheese, typically with added meat, fish, or vegetables. "a cheese and tomato pizza"

Burger

A dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury ingredient, that is fried or grilled and served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

Sandwich

A sandwich is a popular and versatile food item consisting of one or more layers of ingredients placed between slices of bread or a bread-like product, such as a roll, bun, pita, or wrap.
